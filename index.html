PARTICIPANT
INFORMATION SHEET

You are invited to participate in a research study by International St. Mary's Hospital. Please read the information below carefully before deciding whether to participate.

1Purpose of the Study

This study examines healthcare experiences and cardiovascular risk profiles among American residents (active-duty service members, dependents, and civilians) living in Korea. Findings will be used for peer-reviewed academic publication.

2Procedures

You will complete an anonymous online questionnaire (approximately 8 minutes) covering demographics, healthcare experience, self-reported biometrics, and lifestyle factors. After submission you will see a personalized non-diagnostic health profile estimate.

3Risks & Discomforts

There are no foreseeable physical, psychological, or social risks from participating. Some items may feel personal; you may skip any item or stop at any time.

4Benefits

You will receive a personalized non-diagnostic health profile (heart age, brain age, 10-year cardiovascular risk estimate) and a suggested screening plan. Your participation also contributes to research on international resident health in Korea.

5Confidentiality & Anonymity

No identifying information will be collected. We do not record your name, e-mail, phone number, IP address, military ID, or unit designation. All responses are stored anonymously and accessible only to the research team. Aggregate data may be published in academic journals or presented at conferences; individual responses cannot be traced back to you.

6Voluntary Participation

Participation is entirely voluntary. You may decline to participate or withdraw at any time before submission by simply closing this page. There are no penalties or consequences for declining. Once your response is submitted it cannot be withdrawn, because it is not linked to your identity.
